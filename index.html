Processing是什么？｜ About Processing

Processing是一个免费的开源软件平台，为使用代码创作视觉艺术作品提供了一种语言。自2001年以来，成千上万的学生、艺术家、设计师、研究人员和业余爱好者使用Processing来学习和制作原型。这一平台的各种版本（如p5.js的网页版本，Python中的Processing.py，以及Processing for Android，和Processing for Pi ）由 The Processing Foundation 维护。

Processing is a free and open-source software platform, which provides a language for learning how to code within the context of the visual arts. Since 2001, tens of thousands of students, artists, designers, researchers, and hobbyists use Processing for learning and prototyping. Different implementations of this platform (including p5.js, Processing.py in Python, Processing for Android, and Processing for Pi) are maintained by The Processing Foundation. Application deadline is Feb 9, 2020.

Processing Community Day (PCD)是什么？｜About Processing Community Day

更多关于2020年世界各地举办的PCD｜ Learn more about 2020 PCD@worldwide

Processing Community Day是一个庆祝 艺术x代码x多样性 的活动,会在每年的1月—2月期间在世界各地举办。

Processing Community Day is a day to celebrate art, code, and diversity, which will be hold all over the world in January and February.

在麻省理工学院媒体实验室举办的第一次PCD 图片来源 Processing Foundation

Processing community day at MIT Media Lab photo credit by Processing Foundation

第一次PCD于2017年由 Taeyoon Choi 和Processing Foundation于MIT Media Lab（麻省理工学院媒体实验室）举办。我们在南京举办这样的活动是想将Processing Foundation的理念 传递给更多多样化社群，促进视觉艺术与科技之间的融合。

The first Processing Community Day (PCD) was organized by Taeyoon Choi and the Processing Foundation in 2017 at the MIT Media Lab. By organizing a PCD event at Naanjing, we aim to promote software literacy within the visual arts and visual literacy within technology, as well as making these fields more accessible to diverse communities (Check out the Mission of Processing Foundation). 

活动形式｜Forms of Activities

快闪分享｜在5分钟之内快速地以幻灯片的形式高效地呈现你和你的艺术与科技结合的作品吧！

讲座｜20-30分钟内，你就是舞台的主人！详细地介绍你正在做的项目或是你以往的得意之作吧！

工作坊｜45-60分钟内，教大家写一个简单的小程序吧！或者你可以带着自己已经做好的小程序，让更多人参与到基于你的程序的艺术创作中来！

小型作品展示｜预计活动现场会有一个小型的展览空间呈现嘉宾的优秀作品 如果你无法到场却想展出自己的作品，告诉我们如何安装吧！

*抱歉我们在社区日当天不接受任何形式产品及公司的宣传

Show & tell | Introduce yourself and present your works combining arts and technology in 5 minutes.

Keynote Talk | Be the center of the stage and explain your pasts works and the work you are currently working on in 20-30 minutes.

Workshop | Teach others how to write a beginner-friendly program or invite others to make artworks based on your already-made programs.

Showcase | Exhibit your works as an invited speaker. Or if you couldn’t make it to attend the community day, send us your works and detailed instructions so that we can do the setup for you.

*We’re sorry that we don’t accept any form of business promotion on the community day.
